Apple Strudel

Ingredients

  • 4 apples (sliced)
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup raisins (optional)
  • 1/4 cup nuts (optional)
  • 1 sheet phyllo or puff pastry
  • 2 tbsp breadcrumbs
  • Melted butter

Recipe

  1. Mix apples, sugar, cinnamon, raisins, and nuts.
  2. Lay pastry, brush with butter, sprinkle breadcrumbs.
  3. Add filling, roll up, and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 35–40 min.
  4. Dust with sugar and serve warm.

Bostock

Ingredients

  • Sliced brioche
  • Almond cream (frangipane)
  • Butter
  • Powdered sugar
  • Sliced almonds

Recipe

  1. Spread almond cream over brioche slices.
  2. Top with almonds and powdered sugar.
  3. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 12–15 minutes until golden.
  4. Cool slightly and serve.

Crème Caramel

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar (for caramel)
  • 2 cups milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Recipe

  1. Caramelize sugar in a pan and pour into ramekins.
  2. Mix milk, eggs, sugar, and vanilla. Pour into ramekins.
  3. Bake in water bath at 325°F (160°C) for 35–40 min.
  4. Chill, then unmold to serve.

Éclair

Ingredients

  • Choux pastry (butter, flour, eggs, water)
  • Pastry cream
  • Chocolate glaze

Recipe

  1. Pipe choux into logs and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25–30 min.
  2. Cool and fill with pastry cream.
  3. Dip tops in melted chocolate glaze.
  4. Chill before serving.

KitKat Truffle

Ingredients

  • Crushed KitKats
  • Cream cheese
  • Chocolate (for coating)

Recipe

  1. Mix crushed KitKats and cream cheese.
  2. Form balls and chill for 30 mins.
  3. Dip in melted chocolate and let set.
  4. Serve chilled.

Molten Lava Cake

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 4 oz dark chocolate
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup flour

Recipe

  1. Melt butter and chocolate together.
  2. Stir in sugar, eggs, and flour.
  3. Pour into greased ramekins and bake at 425°F (220°C) for 12–14 min.
  4. Serve immediately for molten center.

Paris Brest

Ingredients

  • Choux pastry
  • Praline cream
  • Sliced almonds

Recipe

  1. Pipe choux pastry into a ring shape and top with almonds.
  2. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30–35 min.
  3. Cool and fill with praline cream.
  4. Chill before serving.
